# What are Radiants?

This portion is for those who have not read the books and want an understanding of how the magic system that originates on Roshar works.

Becoming a Radiant requires swearing yourself to an ideal, similar to a paladin in D&D. The type of idea, and a bit of the personality of the person, will determine the radiant order. For example, someone who isn't very outgoing and possibly selfish **could not** become a [Windrunner](#windrunners), but they could be a [Lightweaver](#lightweavers)

When swearing this ideal the person is doing this with a spren, and that spren (the DM in this case) must accept those words. Which does mean that you can not just say the words, you must **feel** them, and have grown to be them.

## What Radiant Orders Are Available to Play?

There are 10 orders of Radiants, with 9 of them available to play. [Bondsmiths](#bondsmiths) are the only that are not available to actually play, although they are in the handbook for descriptive and world building purposes.

| Order                           | Spren            | Surges                            | Philosophy                                  |
|---------------------------------|------------------|-----------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| [Windrunners](#windrunners)     | Honorspren       | Adhesion and Gravitation          | Protect the Innocent and the defenseless    |
| [SkyBreakers](#skybreakers)     | Highspren        | Gravitation and Division          | Enforce the law and strive for justice.     |
| [Dustbringers](#dustbringers)   | Ashspren         | Division and Abrasion             | Great power requires strong disipline       |
| [Edgedancers](#edgedancers)     | Cultivationspren | Abrasion and Progression          | Remember and serve those who others forget. |
| [Truthwatchers](#truthwatchers) | Mistspren        | Progression and Illumination      | Search for fundamental truth and share it.  |
| [Lightweavers](#lightweaaversm) | Cryptic          | Illumination and Transformation   | Separate truthe from lies.                  |
| [Elsecallers](#elsecallers)     | Inkspren         | Transformation and Transportation | Strive to reach your true potential         |
| [Willshapers](#willshapers)     | Lightspren       | Transportation and Cohesion       | Seek freedom and choice for all peoples     |
| [Stonewards](#stonewards)       | Peakspren        | Cohesion and Tension              | Be the support on which others can depend.  |
